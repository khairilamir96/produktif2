Context or Background

The "Halal Market" is driven by more than 1.8 billion global muslims around the world, and the increasing number of halal consumers from non-mMuslim population, placing the industry on an exponential growth. Halal industry contributes an annual export value of Rm40 billion for halal products, approximately 4% of the country's total exports. Halal Development Corporation (HDC) established on 18 September 2006, spearheads the development of Malaysia's integrated and comprehensive halal ecosystem and infrastructure to position Malaysia as the most competitive country leading the global halal industry. HDC an agency under the Ministry of INternational Trade and INdustry (MITI), it is the world's first Government-backed halal industry development corporation
